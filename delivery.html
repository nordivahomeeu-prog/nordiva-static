Delivery & Shipping

We deliver our premium furniture across Europe. Here's everything you need to know about our shipping process.

🚚 Free Delivery

Free shipping on all orders over €500 within Europe

📦 Shipping Times

Standard Delivery: 7-14 business days

Express Delivery: 5-10 business days (additional fee)

Custom Orders: 3-6 weeks depending on specifications

🌍 Delivery Areas

We ship to all EU countries including:

  • Germany, France, Italy, Spain, Netherlands
  • Belgium, Austria, Poland, Czech Republic
  • Sweden, Denmark, Finland, Norway
  • And all other European countries

💳 Payment Options

Cash on Delivery: Pay when you receive your order

Bank Transfer: Secure payment via SEPA transfer

Credit Card: Visa, Mastercard accepted

Delivery Process

  1. Place your order via Instagram DM
  2. We confirm availability and delivery date
  3. Your furniture is carefully packaged
  4. Shipped with tracking information
  5. Delivered to your door with assembly instructions

Returns & Warranty

All our products come with a 2-year warranty. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, you can return it within 14 days of delivery. Items must be in original condition and packaging.
